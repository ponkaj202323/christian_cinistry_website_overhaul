The Parable of the Sower

“A sower went out to sow. And as he sowed, some seed fell along the path, and the birds came and devoured it.”

“Other seed fell on rocky ground, where it did not have much soil, and immediately it sprang up, since it had no depth of soil. But when the sun rose, it was scorched. And since it had no root, it withered away.”
